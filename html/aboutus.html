About Us

At MyMiniFactory, we have a confession to make: we're 3D printing enthusiasts through and through. The technology fascinates us, inspires us, and drives us to create and innovate every day. And that's exactly why we founded MyMiniFactory in 2013: to share our passion for 3D printing with the world and create a platform for like-minded individuals to connect and collaborate. When we first started out, we were a small team of designers, engineers, and makers who believed in the power of 3D printing to transform the way we make things. We wanted to create a platform where anyone could share their designs and ideas, regardless of their skill level or background. And so, we launched MyMiniFactory as a free platform for sharing and downloading 3D designs.

As our community of users grew, we quickly realized that there was a need for a more robust and curated selection of 3D designs. We saw talented designers struggling to make a living from their work, and we knew that we could do something to help. And so, we pivoted our platform to focus on high-quality, premium 3D designs, with a marketplace where designers could showcase their work and be compensated for their talent and hard work.

Over the years, we've worked tirelessly to build the best possible platform for 3D printing enthusiasts. We've expanded our offerings to include a range of 3D printing products, including filaments, printers, and accessories, and we've partnered with some of the biggest names in the industry to bring our customers the latest and greatest technology.

But we've never lost sight of our core mission: to build a community of makers and designers who share our passion for 3D printing. We've sponsored events, hackathons, and competitions, and we've worked closely with educators to bring 3D printing into the classroom. We've built a vibrant and supportive community of makers, designers, and enthusiasts from around the world, and we're proud of the amazing things that they've accomplished using our platform.

As we look back on the journey of MyMiniFactory, we are filled with gratitude for the incredible community that has grown around our platform. We are constantly inspired by the amazing designs and innovations that our users create every day, and we are humbled by the impact that 3D printing has had on so many people's lives. But we also know that there is so much more that we can do. The potential of 3D printing is limitless, and we believe that by working together and pushing the boundaries of what's possible, we can continue to change the world for the better.
